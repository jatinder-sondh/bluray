About Us

Welcome to Blu Ray Concreting Services

ONE OF THE LARGEST CONCRETING SERVICE PROVIDERS IN MELBOURNE (AUSTRALIA), SERVING BOTH COMMERCIAL AND RESIDENTIAL CLIENTS.

Being in the concrete finishing service for the past five years, we furnish our clients with the perfect finishing at the Footings, Excavations & soil removal, Rock breaking and so much more. We ensure that we deliver your project on time and as per your requirements!

Blu Ray Concreting has evolved as one of the largest concreting service providers in Australia, serving both commercial and residential clients in the northeast and western suburbs of Melbourne, Australia since its inception five years back. The company was founded in 2017 by Mr. Gurinder. Since then, he & his team have continued the tradition of excellence until today.

Our dedicated staff has the expertise to handle all the concrete and excavation-related projects For excavating, we perform Digging, Earth Moving, Land Clearing, Earthwork, and Topsoil Removal, Rock Breaking and Rock Removal services to make an area ready for construction or below-ground service activities.

Over the years, our dedication towards furnishing quality work, our commitment to work on 100% client satisfaction, and our involvement in the local community have earned us a reputation we feel proud of.

We accommodate every job assigned to us in the best possible manner. With this, we are obliged to extend our gratitude towards our previous clients including construction companies, infrastructure companies, contractors, real estate developers, Industrial Projects (Steel, Cement, Power Plants, etc.), commercial projects along with individual owners, who still believe in us and return to use our services for their future projects over and over again.

We look forward to many more years in keeping our pace high to impart the same level of authenticity and perfection in our upcoming projects and stay ahead of everyone in providing exquisite concreting services to new and returning clients alike.
